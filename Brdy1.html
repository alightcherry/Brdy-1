Shravya… na life lo nuvvu vachina roju nundi everything change ayyindi. Before you, life just simple ga nadustundi… but after you came, every moment special ayyindi. Nee smile chusthe na mind lo unna stress motham disappear ayipothundi. Nuvvu simple ga "hi" annappudu kuda na day complete ayipothundi Papa. Sometimes nenu alochistha… ela oka person na life ni intha beautiful ga marchagaladu ani. Answer okkate — adhi **Shravya**. Nuvvu matladithe na heart calm avuthundi. Nuvvu navvithe na world bright avuthundi. Nuvvu sad ga unte na heart kuda heavy aipothundi.

Papa… life lo chala mandi vastharu, veltharu. But kontha mandi matram mana life lo permanent ga mark leave chestharu. For me that person is **you**. Nenu perfect person kaakapovachu. Na daggara flaws kuda untayi. But okka promise matram chestha — ninnu happy ga chudataniki na best ivvadaniki always try chestha. Because for me… love ante just word kaadu. Love ante **Shravya**. Nee smile protect cheyadam, nee dreams support cheyadam, nee side lo life motham nilabadadam — idhi na heart lo unna real wish.

Papa… nenu future gurinchi alochinappudu na mind lo automatically oka scene vastundi. Nuvvu navvuthu na pakkana nadusthunna scene. Mana madhya chinna chinna fights. Malli patch up ayi navvukune moments. Simple ga cheppali ante — **I want you in every chapter of my life story.** Because without you life incomplete ga feel avuthundi. And today nenu oka small question adagali anukuntunna…
